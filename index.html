"NayePankh Foundation" is a non governmental organisation. We are a team of students who provide a platform to the youth to utilize their energy for the growth of the nation.

Our focus is on education, health, and holistic development. By bridging the gap between privilege and poverty, we ensure that every child gets their "New Wings" (NayePankh) to fly towards a brighter future.
