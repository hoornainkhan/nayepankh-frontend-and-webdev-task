Who we are & Why we exist

Driven by the energy of youth and a shared vision of a dignified life for all, we are redefining grassroots social change in India.

How it started?

The 2020 Genesis

Amidst the global uncertainty of COVID-19, a small group of passionate high school students witnessed the silent struggles of daily wagers. What started as a simple desire to distribute food packets turned into a movement that couldn't be contained by four walls.

2020
Growth

A Growing Collective

Word spread. High schoolers became college students, and the team grew from 5 to 50, and then to hundreds. We realized that youth energy, if channeled properly, could solve systematic issues rather than just temporary ones.

28th March 2021

NayePankh Foundation was officially registered and launched. A new flight began, formalized and ready to scale impact across the nation. This marked our commitment to professional accountability and long-term service.
